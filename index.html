Research on Ancient Bronze Classification and Dating Algorithms

By integrating features such as contour, vessel shape, and decorative patterns, an end-to-end multi-task model for the classification and dating of ancient bronzes was constructed, and a bronze artifact category-function matrix was designed to assist the classification task. Ablation experiments demonstrated that the classification accuracy reached 97.59% , and the dating accuracy reached 87.57% .

Research on Panoramic Image Quality Assessment Algorithms

A subjective experiment was designed, organizing 16 participants to rate 3,200 viewport images, thereby constructing a dataset.
